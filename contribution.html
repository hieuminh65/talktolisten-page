Talk To Listen is part of ACM projects

What are ACM projects?

It is an initiative led by ACM tech team aimed at fostering innovation and collaboration within our community. We welcome members of all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned professionals, to come together and work on projects, learn new technical skills and test them out. Whether you have a brilliant idea you’re itching to bring to life or are eager to contribute to ongoing projects, ACM projects offers the perfect opportunity

How to join ACM projects?

To become a part of ACM projects and embark on your journey of tech innovation, it’s as simple as reaching out to our dedicated project leads. If you’re interested in hardware projects, you can contact our hardware lead, Harsh Sahrawat, on discord or harshs@usf.edu. For software enthusiasts feel free to get in touch with our software lead, Vatsal Kapoor, on discord or kapoor115@usf.edu

For Talk To Listen, you can contact our project lead, Hieu Nguyen, here or to hieung.tech@gmail.com. There are many tasks that need to be done, so feel free to reach out. Welcome to the team!
