Talk To Listen is part of ACM projects

What are ACM projects?

It is an initiative led by ACM tech team aimed at fostering innovation and collaboration within our community. We welcome members of all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned professionals, to come together and work on projects, learn new technical skills and test them out. Whether you have a brilliant idea you’re itching to bring to life or are eager to contribute to ongoing projects, ACM projects offers the perfect opportunity

How to join ACM projects?

To become a part of ACM projects and embark on your journey of tech innovation, it’s as simple as reaching out to our dedicated project leads. If you’re interested in hardware projects, you can contact our hardware lead, Harsh Sahrawat, on discord or harshs@usf.edu. For software enthusiasts feel free to get in touch with our software lead, Vatsal Kapoor, on discord or kapoor115@usf.edu

How to join Talk To Listen?

For Talk To Listen, you can contact and send your resume to our project lead, Hieu Nguyen, here or to hieung.tech@gmail.com. There are many tasks that need to be done, so feel free to reach out

We're excited to welcome you to our project, where learners of all skill levels can jump in and get their hands dirty with some real-world app development. It doesn't matter how much experience you have – if you're eager to learn new things and excited about our app, you're in the right place! We can guide you and grow your skills. We welcome and encourage you to bring fresh, creative ideas and innovative solutions to the team. Your unique perspectives and problem-solving skills are valuable assets that can drive the success of our project. Here, you have the freedom to experiment, challenge the status quo, and contribute meaningfully

Front-End Developer: In this role, you will work in collaboration with our design team to implement the user interface of our app using React Native

UI / UX Desiner: We are looking for a UI/UX Designer to lead the design of our app's interface using tools such as Figma. Responsibilities include creating user-centric designs, as well as developing visual assets for marketing and app store and google play store presentations.

Back-End Developer: As a Back-End Developer, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining our app’s server infrastructure using FastAPI, a modern Python framework to build APIs. You will implement and deploy multiple APIs endpoints to provide a robust app's infrastructure

Machine Learning / AI Engineer: You’ll research and integrate cutting-edge open-source ML models, enhancing our app’s capabilities and user experience
